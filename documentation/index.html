To configure this form, Open "qcForm.php" located inside . . /assets/form/qcForm.php. It looks something like this : 

// Replace your@email.com 
$mailTo = 'your@email.com';

if ($_POST['formtype'] == 'contact-form') {
	$firstname = $_POST['firstname'];
	$email = $_POST['email'];
	$subject = ('Contact Form').' [ '.$_SERVER["HTTP_HOST"].' ] ';
	$message = $_POST['message'];
	$body = ("Name: $firstname \n\nEmail: $email \n\nMessage: $message" );
}

$headers = ('From:') .' <'.$firstname.'>' . "\r\n" . ('Reply-To:') . $email;
mail($mailTo, $subject, $body, $headers);
& Replace "your@email.com" with your email id. This is the Email ID where you will receive all incoming mails.

How To's Some of the frequently asked questions for newbies

  • 1. How to change body's Fullscreen Background ?

    => Open style.css & Under *Global tag (on Line no : 74) replace "http://www.placehold.it/1280x1024&text=background+Image" with your Background Image.


  • 2. How to configure Homepage Fullscreen Slideshow ?

    => Open index.html & Under head tag (onwards Line no : 50) replace Slides Images with your Background Images.


  • 3. How to configure Homepage Audio ?

    => Open index.html & Under head tag (onwards Line no : 30) replace .mp3 & .ogg source files with your respective .mp3 & .ogg source files.


  • 3. How to configure Audio Playlist on album-single.html?

    => Open album-single.html & Under head tag (onwards Line no : 36). You need to repeat below script for each track you want to include in the playlist. 

    			{
